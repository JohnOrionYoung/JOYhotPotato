FAQ

Quick Start

Install the Metamask browser extension and use it to purchase Ethereum from Coinbase using a credit card. Buy art from the JOY Art Market. Profit when someone buys a work out of your collection. Earn a percentage of every transaction while you hold work in your collection.

JOY

John Orion Young (JOY) is a contemporary artist living in San Francisco. Classically trained from childhood in his father's art studio in painting and sculpting, JOY's mediums today have evolved to include virtual reality, blockchain, and AI.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized software platform that enables Smart Contracts and Distributed Applications (ĐApps) to be built and run without any downtime, fraud, control or interference from a third party. We use an Ethereum ERC-721 Smart Contract to create a unique token to represent the value of each individual artwork. The tokens can then be freely traded between collectors without the interference of galleries, museums, or other third parties.

Web 3.0

Metamask is a bridge that allows you to visit the distributed web of tomorrow in your browser today. It enables you to run Ethereum ĐApps right in your browser without running a full Ethereum node. Android and iOS users can access the distributed web through a mobile ĐApp browser like Trust or Toshi.

ĐApp

ĐApps allow total transparency of ownership, a ledger of every transaction, and remove the need for 3rd party interaction. This means that if you buy a work of art, no one can just turn off the website and take it away from you. It's yours, you can even link to the smart contract and make your own site for your collection.

Buying

Click the blue buy button once you find a work of art you love. This will trigger the Metamask popup. Check the numbers and click "Submit". Approval should take a few seconds to minutes. Then poof, you become a sophisticated art collector. Remember before you buy that this is a game of trading tokens that represent works of art on the blockchain. Please keep your head and never spend more than you can afford to lose.

Selling

The moment a work of art becomes yours, the price is set by the Smart Contract for the next buyer. This guarantees you a nice profit if the work is bought from you. Lower prices have smaller jumps so the work may flip quicker. At higher prices, it is easier to hold a work of art in your collection longer.

Fee

We charge a 10% fee on each transaction for development and art work. This enables us to start the artworks sale price at just 0.001 ETH. Every purchase you make inide our art exchange also helps us towards building JOYworld. A magical creative space in virtual reality where you will be able to experience the art you own.

XReality

All of the art is created by John Orion Young in virtual reality with the intended experience to be in augmented, mixed, and virtual reality. It's rendered and then the render file is deleted. This process from virtual reality tools, rendering, and uploading straight to the blockchain creates an artwork with no physical trace that is perfect for token based ownership.
